About LITHIUM 2K26

LITHIUM 2K26 is the most anticipated freshers' festival of the year, designed to welcome the new batch with open arms and unforgettable experiences. This one-day extravaganza will set the tone for your incredible journey ahead.

Prepare to be dazzled by performances, inspired by speakers, and connected with your peers through a series of exciting events carefully crafted to celebrate your arrival at our prestigious institution.
